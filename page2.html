Verbal Bullying

Why It Should STOP!!!

How It Happens

Verbal bullying is one of the easiest forms of bullying.It is also one of the most common and many kids and teens experience it. Different ways of verbal bullying include name-calling, teasing, insults, spreading rumors and gossip, intimidation and threats, and racist or sexist comments For more information, please click here.

Effects of Verbal Bullying

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Shyness

How To Solve It!

  1. Surround yourself with people who love you and make you feel confident.
  2. Tell a parent, teacher, counselor, or trusted adult about what's going on.
