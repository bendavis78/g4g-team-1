Physical Bullying

1 in 4 teachers don't find bullying a problem and are fine with bullying. A minimum of 13 million students are bullied a year in the United States.

How to make it stop!

  • Be with a friend at all times.
  • Walk away when the bully attempts to harm you.
  • Tell an adult about the bullying even if it makes you look like a snitch.
  • Dont react to the attempts-bullies often want the reaction you give them.
