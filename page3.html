Talking About It

Ahh!! You are being bullied and its gone too far. Its time to get a adult invoved, but there is one problem.... You Don't Know How!!

3 Easy Steps

  1. Be straight! Tell the whole story! The need to know who, what, where, when, and why! Don't be chicken!
  2. Give them the whole story;if your not specific they may not belive you.
  3. Ask for help! Don't be shy, speak your mind

3 Things NOT to Do

  1. Never Ever lie about being bullied. It never ends well.
  2. If you shrug it off then you only hurt yourself, so dont back off!
  3. Don't blow up, take what your parents give you and work with it.

Remeber there are differnt tipes of bulling. Be sure to tell them witch one it is.
