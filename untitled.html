Physical Bullying

1 in 4 teachers don't find bullying a problem and are fine with bullying. A minimum of 13 million students are bullied a year in the United States Physical bullying includes hitting, kicking, tripping, pushing, or anything that causes physical harm to the body or possesions of the bullies target.

How to make it stop!

  • Be with a friend at all times.
  • Walk away when the bully attempts to harm you.
  • Tell an adult about the bullying even if it makes you look like a snitch.
  • Dont react to the attempts-bullies often want the reaction you give them.
