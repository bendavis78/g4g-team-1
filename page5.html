Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying is bullying on the internet and social media. Sites like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn are the perfect places for cyberbullying. It's very similar to verbal bullying in the sense that, instead of the bully saying something, they're posting it on social media. Rude texts, photos, messages, emails, comments, and posts are all forms of cyberbullying. It is one of the leading causes of depression and suicide among teenagers. It can happen to ANYONE. If you're being bullied, or you are a bully, Cyberbullying.us has many good resources and tips for help.
